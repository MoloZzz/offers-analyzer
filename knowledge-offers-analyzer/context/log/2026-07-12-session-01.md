## Storage — confirmed, with two honest nuances

We DO persist everything fetched: `ListingsService.recordSeen()` upserts `listings` + appends `price_observations`; plus `fair_value_benchmarks` (cached RIA average per cohort), `opportunities`, `notifications`. Nuances the user rightly flagged:
1. **Fair value today = RIA average-price API**, not computed from our own stored rows. Storage currently powers dedup + history and enables the *planned* own-median/percentile valuation (more robust than a single average).
2. **US1 fetches details only for NEW ids** (budget saving), so known listings aren't re-observed → price history is mostly first-seen; price-drop (FR-009) + re-observation are later (polish/US2). To compute price movements / own market stats over time we must periodically re-observe known listings, which competes for the 30/hr budget.
